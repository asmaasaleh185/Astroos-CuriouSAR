WHO WE ARE

We are Astroos, a team of young innovators driven by curiosity and guided by data. Our project, "Echoes of Water, Shadows of Loss: Derna Seen by Radar," was created for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025 under the theme Through the Radar Looking Glass.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to turn complex satellite data into meaningful stories. Using NASA's open data and resources, we transform Sentinel-1 Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery into an accessible visual and educational experience. By doing so, we make it easier for everyone‚Äînot just scientists‚Äîto understand how radar "sees" the Earth beyond clouds and darkness.
